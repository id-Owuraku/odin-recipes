Fries

Description

Thinly cut and deep-fried potato sticks, known for their crispy exterior and fluffy interior. They are a popular side dish and often enjoyed with ketchup or other dipping sauces.

Ingredients

  • Yam
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Wash and cut the yam.
  2. Soak the yam.
  3. Heat the oil.
  4. Fry the yam.
  5. Drain and cool the fries.
