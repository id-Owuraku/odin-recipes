Lo-mein

Description

Lo Mein is a stir-fried noodle dish originating from China. It's known for its savory flavors and customizable with various vegetables and proteins.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Vegetables
  • Protein (optional)
  • Soy sauce
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles.
  2. Marinate the protein.
  3. Prepare the vegetables.
  4. Cook the protein.
  5. Combine everything and stir-fry
